The time I went the sky couldn’t possibly get more foggy!
This was the site I was aiming for when I set off on my bike yesterday (Day 18 – 6/04/19) I originally thought you could ride to the top by small windy roads but that’s not the case. It is possible but they don’t allow it (Correct me I’m wrong)
I’m actually glad I couldn’t ride up there as I ended up experiencing the highlight of my trip! The Ba Na Hills Cable Car *** Longest in the world + top 10 cable car routes on earth, as featured by CNN ***
👉 https://www.facebook.com/154943102119258/posts/163611951252373?sfns=xmwa
It was busy 12:30pm, I didn’t time my visit there properly if I was to avoid the mass crowds and on reflection….yeah I would have got the shots with hardly anyone on the bridge, but you can’t tell with the weather. That would also mean being ready to cable car it at 7am! I’m not too fussed it’s all good. The cable car journey was epic and I’ll remember that for ever
You see all those wowing photos of the bridge on the net, but rarely do you see the ones where’s it busy and very cloudy/foggy/misty…..call it what you like
I feel that I still took some goodens and it gives you all a teaser especially for anyone planning to go also
