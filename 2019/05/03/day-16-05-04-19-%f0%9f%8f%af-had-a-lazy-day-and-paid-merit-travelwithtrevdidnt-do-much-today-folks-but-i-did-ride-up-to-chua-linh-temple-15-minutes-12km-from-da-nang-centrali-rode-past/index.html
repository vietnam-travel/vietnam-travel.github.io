Day 16 – 05/04/19 🏯 Had a lazy day and paid merit #travelwithtrev
Didn’t do much today folks but I did ride up to Chua Linh Temple 15 minutes (12km) from Da Nang Central
I rode past here yesterday but my time was short to Temple it + ride to the end of the peninsular
Stunning views over the bay with Da Nangs high rises in the distance. Beautiful temple grounds as you can see….. and going around 4:30pm means you can see the area with blue skies and by 5:45pm the sun is starting to set with the sky alight!
I’m off to pick up a motorcross bike this evening and tomorrow I ride to Hue, which was once Vietnam’s capital city. The journey will take me on the Hai Van Pass, made famous and featured on Top Gear back in 2008. Really looking forward to this and I highly doubt it will be a disappointment
To follow my travels from south to north https://www.facebook.com/whereeverhesat/
