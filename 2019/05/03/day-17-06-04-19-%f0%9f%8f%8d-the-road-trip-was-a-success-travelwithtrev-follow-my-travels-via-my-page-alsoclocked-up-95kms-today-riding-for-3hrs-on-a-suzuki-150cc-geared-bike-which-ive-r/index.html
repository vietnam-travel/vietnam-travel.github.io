Day 17 – 06/04/19 🏍 The road trip was a success #travelwithtrev follow my travels via my page also
Clocked up 95kms today riding for 3hrs+ on a Suzuki 150cc geared bike which I’ve rented for 3 days. The bike was provided by ‘Da Nang Motorbike rental & tours’
I started off from Da Nang Central at 10am and rode to the Ba Na Hills cable car….then went up to the FAMOUS Golden Hands Bridge ***Pics + write up to follow***
After I came back down the next destination was Hoi Ann. I made my way but looping the whole area around Hoi Ann before and after I arrived there …..coming on and off the main roads all afternoon
The bike was half a decent ride but it’s painful riding in Vietnam….as you have to be extremely defensive when riding. You must assume every Tom, Dick (Many of them) and Harry is going to pull out or come to close ending you. I managed to get the bike (Safe road, confident situation) up to 125kmph probably another 15 tops
I’ve literally just arrived in the ancient town (7pm) as I was putting the bike to good use and really seeing the area
I haven’t pre booked one single hotel online since I’ve been in Vietnam…. always waking in random joints and going from there. Tonight was no different
My ass & back hurts a bit….my right hand is a bit numb and I need a power nap and massage
