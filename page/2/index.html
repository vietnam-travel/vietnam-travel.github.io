🇻🇳 Day 23 – 12/04/09 🐊 A cursed waterpark with a history of roaming crocodiles, location unmapped to a traveler, government stated ‘NO ACCESS ALLOWED’ and of course…….bribery 💵
To be honest I hadn’t done any research on what to see and do in Hue and the surrounding area what so ever. I was here because of the ride through the Hai Van Pass featured in the TOP GEAR series
I woke up today at 11am, got the coffee in and slapped myself a few times to wake up, then googled the top things to see in Hue and scrolled through
The Imperial palace complex + a bagful of temples etc popped up but what caught my attention was of course THE ABANDONDED WATERPARK, which this post is all about! (Don’t forget to click the links to the 3x vids I took)
I had a quick read on up online with my hand over my face, with a small opening to see the location and any straight off important pre Info I needed to know (Didn’t want any spoiler pics of details to ruin my experience) it turns out I was kind of familiar already with the Waterpark….I recall seeing the place featured in an online article a few years back of the top 20 abandoned places on earth
Well there you go….. it was my time…. by full on coincidence!
It was short ride only 15 minutes from Hue Central (9km)
As I approached the abandoned old carpark and entrance I was greeted by a security guard (I’ll call him Steve for this post) Steve wanted 50k Dong to go in….. I paused for a few seconds and held back before committing….I went online to see what was what again and read mixed comments on how to get in
Some said you’re no longer allowed, every day Is different, a few would say park up a few hundred meters away and jungle it in, or an option of paying 30k to ride in and around, and finally park up for 5k and entrance 10k…. all comments were varied over different dates from the past 5 years
Well screw this…. if I keep reading it will be dark! I couldn’t be arsed spending all day so I looked around quickly….when another chap said I could park up for 10k then it was 20k to walk in. Fair enough I’ll take that option (Pennies anyway) and I wanted to go walkabout anyway not ride in
Steve pointed me down a jungle path left of the entrance….but the google map was showing that doing a straight right would take me down the old main entrance road and towards the lake, where I planned to do a full loop of the area, checking out every nuke and cranny. I didn’t come to get a few photos and back in to town… I planned to scope this turf good and proper
I got a slow stride on and off I went….. saw a few grazing cows and stray dogs, which I I tried to pal up with (Dogs of course) but they weren’t haven’t any of it…. fine!! Their loss…. I always give out good dog massages, they will be gutted if they ever find out!
A bridge leading to a large building/arena area was my first spot….. it was covered in graffiti and not a sole about
Sorry peeps!!!!!! That’s it for now….I Will update more tomorrrow 🤝 Pop back tomoz and I’ll finish the rest

You can take a bus, taxi, grab or just go by yourself and enjoy the motorbike ride.😎
