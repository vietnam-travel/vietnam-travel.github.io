Day 15 – 04/04/19 🛵 That was fabulous ride! #travelwithtrev
Didn’t leave Da Nang and I’m still renting scooters….but trust me I wouldn’t be for a long trip or off road, no no no!
I have got a 250cc Motorcross lined up for tomorrow as I’m off roading and will be clocking up some kms over the next few days!
This afternoon I visited the Marble Mountains 25minutes (11km) ride from Da Nang Central 👉 https://www.facebook.com/154943102119258/posts/162834517996783?sfns=xmwa …..then finished the day with a lovely ride to and around the Son Tra peninsula for 2 hours
Sitting in an Indian curry house writing this now…..will wash it down with a few beers then get a hot stone massage and call it a night with a bottle or red Chile wine 🍷
