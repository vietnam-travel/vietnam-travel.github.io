Dear members,
🚩 You prefer to spend your day in a place with no sign of tourists? A place with true local spirit just outside of Hanoi? I found one 10 months ago and decided to stay. Join me for a bicycle ride in my village #Co_Loa relic site and find out why I stay. It’s half way from Old Quater to the airport. I’ll work on your schedule checking in/out hotel or train/ flight to fit everything nicely
⬇
🚴 3 hrs riding through village covering in green fields and garden surrounded by Coloa Citadel mud wall system dated back more than 2000 years ago, seeing real life of locals with rich historical value, interacting with locals, sitting back with cold beer at the heart of the village next to lotus lake watching villagers passing by. Totally easy going and laid back time
♨ Meal following lunch/dinner. Food is plentiful coz I’m a cookery teacher and I love cooking. Ingredients collected freshly from the farmers. Vegeterian option is available
✔ Price is VND 400k/pax only for this group members . Bicycles, temple ticket, snacks, 5 course meal. Grab taxi costs around VND 150k each turn
✔ This is the only bicycle route in this village, unknown to tourists. Only 20′ drive from Old Quater, half way to the airport. It’s perfect to spend time and enjoy your meal before catching your flight
✅ I can arrange scooter for free if you cannot ride the pushbike
✅ Medium reading with piper betle leaf might available if you are into spiritual world as part of experience
PM me to book/for more questions
